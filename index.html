An Ode to his Mediocrity

There once was a lad

He held only a single gonad

Thus, his seed was rather slow

A fertile lass he may never know

Yet, a plan he did hatch

It came to him one night as he masturbated to his Cabbage Patch

For he was never one to fret

His elequent plan: to hump a baguette

I wish I could tell you his plan went through

Unfortunately, he died from huffing glue
